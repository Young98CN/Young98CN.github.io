Biography [CV]

My name is Weijie Ma (Wilkie Ma)/马炜杰/馬煒傑. I am currently a first-year MPhil student in the School of Science and Engineering, The Chinese University of Hong Kong, Shenzhen, supervised by Prof. Ruimao Zhang and Prof. Zhen Li. Previously, I received the B. Eng degree from School of Information Engineering, Zhengzhou University in 2021.

My research interest lies in deep learning and computer vision, including scene understanding, multi-modal learning, and medical image analysis.
